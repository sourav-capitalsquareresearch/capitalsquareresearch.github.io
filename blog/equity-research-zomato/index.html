Executive Summary

Equity Research | Zomato Ltd.

Investment Thesis

Zomato has evolved from a simple food discovery platform into a dominant hyperlocal logistics giant. Our bullish outlook is predicated on the successful integration and rapid scaling of **Blinkit**, which has transformed Zomato from a single-engine growth story into a diversified platform powerhouse.

Key Driver 1: Margin Expansion

Increasing take-rates and ad-monetization in the food delivery segment are driving double-digit contribution margins.

Key Driver 2: Quick Commerce Moat

Blinkit is achieving unit-level break-even faster than anticipated, leveraging Zomato's massive delivery fleet.

Segment Deep-Dive

Equity Research | Zomato Ltd.

Food Delivery: The Cash Cow

The core food delivery business has stabilized into a duopoly. We expect contribution margins to scale to 7.5% by FY27 as Zomato continues to optimize delivery routes and increase platform fees.

Blinkit: The Growth Engine

Quick commerce is the largest surprise of FY24. Blinkit's GOV (Gross Order Value) is now growing at 3x the rate of food delivery, with dark store density reaching critical mass in Tier-1 cities.

Analyst's Note: The "Network Effect"

"The synergy between Food Delivery and Quick Commerce is undervalued. Zomato's Gold members are spending 40% more on Blinkit than non-members. This cross-pollination reduces CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) significantly, creating a fly-wheel effect that competitors will find hard to replicate."
