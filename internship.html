HIRING FOR SUMMER 2024

Investment Research Intern

Remote • 3-6 Months • Fast-Paced Institutional Environment

Role Overview

Capital Square Research is seeking highly motivated candidates for our Investment Research Internship program. As an intern, you will work directly with senior analysts to monitor high-growth sectors, maintain dynamic financial models, and synthesize alternative datasets. We operate at the intersection of traditional fundamental analysis and automated data extraction. You will not be fetching coffee; you will be analyzing secular trends and building institutional-grade models.

Key Responsibilities

  • ▹

    Build and maintain comprehensive 3-statement financial models and DCF valuations.

  • ▹

    Assist in drafting initiation reports, earnings updates, and sector deep-dives.

  • ▹

    Track industry-specific KPIs across Hospitals, Logistics, Data Centers, and Consumer Durables.

  • ▹

    Extract and clean alternative data points from public filings, transcripts, and industry publications.

Requirements

  • ▹

    Education: Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Finance, Economics, Engineering, Mathematics, or a related quantitative field.

  • ▹

    Technical Skills: Advanced Excel proficiency required. Familiarity with Python, SQL, or data scraping is a significant plus but not mandatory.

  • ▹

    Financial Acumen: Strong grasp of accounting principles, corporate finance, and equity valuation techniques.

  • ▹

    Soft Skills: Extreme attention to detail, exceptional written communication, and the ability to thrive under tight deadlines.

What We Offer

Live Deal Experience

Work on active coverage and produce actionable research consumed by institutional clients.

Mentorship

Direct feedback and 1-on-1 modeling sessions with senior equity research analysts.

Certification

Receive a verifiable certification ID upon successful completion, validating your technical skills.

Flexible Setup

100% remote working environment with flexible hours tailored around your university schedule.
