About Capital Square

Where fundamental analysis meets quantitative rigor.

Mission Statement

To bridge the gap between retail curiosity and institutional-grade analysis by providing transparent, data-driven equity research models for high-growth sectors.

Vision Statement

To become the primary educational resource for aspiring analysts and investors seeking to understand the fundamental drivers of India’s evolving economy, from Hospitals to Data Centers.

What We Do

Deep-Dive Sectoral Research

Granular analysis of industry tailwinds and headwinds.

Fundamental Modeling

Building transparent, logic-based DCF and Relative Valuation models.

Quarterly Earnings Tracking

Summarizing corporate results into actionable insights.

Analyst Training

Mentoring the next generation of researchers through our internship program.

What We Do NOT Do

Financial Advisory

We do not provide "Buy/Sell/Hold" recommendations.

Portfolio Management

We do not manage client funds or provide personalized investment plans.

Intraday/Technical Calls

Our focus is strictly long-term fundamental analysis.

SEBI Registered Services

We do not operate as a registered investment advisor (RIA) or Research Analyst (RA) under SEBI guidelines.

Partner With Us

Interested in our research, seeking advisory services, or want to explore synergies? We are always open to connecting with aligned institutions.
