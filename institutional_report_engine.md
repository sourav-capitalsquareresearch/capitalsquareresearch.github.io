# 🏛️ INSTITUTIONAL_REPORT_ENGINE
## The Capital Square Research Transformation Protocol

This engine is the proprietary architectural standard for **Capital Square Research**. It defines the automated transformation of raw intelligence into world-class, data-dense institutional assets.

---

## 💎 Brand Identity & Core Visuals
Every report generated must radiate the **Capital Square** hallmark: *Precision in Data, Elegance in Design.*

- **The Capital Square Palette**:
  - **Primary (The Void)**: `#060B19` (Deep Navy) — Represents depth and focus.
  - **Accent (The Alpha)**: `#00E676` (Alpha Green) — Represents growth and performance.
  - **Surface**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03)` (Glassmorphism) — Represents transparency.
- **Typography Matrix**:
  - **Headings**: `Montserrat` (Extrabold/Black) — For institutional authority.
  - **Narrative**: `Inter` (Regular/Medium) — For maximum readability.
  - **Intelligence**: `Roboto Mono` (Medium) — For financial data and tickers.
- **Imagery**: 
  - Use high-contrast, dark-themed abstract backgrounds from Unsplash.
  - Always overlay with a `0.8` opacity Navy gradient to ensure legibility.

---

## 📊 Phase 1: Data Intelligence (Excel ➔ HTML)
Financial models are the soul of our research. They must be presented with absolute clarity.

- **Class: `.fin-table-pro`**:
  - **The Header**: Alpha Green background, Montserrat Bold, white text.
  - **Row Logic**: Sub-surface navy (`#0A1128`) alternating with transparent rows.
  - **Formatting**: Right-align all currency; use 2-decimal precision for ratios; commas for thousands.
- **Visual Analytics**:
  - Charts must be interactive and responsive.
  - **Color Strategy**: Primary series in Alpha Green; Benchmarks in Dashed Navy/White; Projections in Gradient Fills.

---

## ✍️ Phase 2: Narrative Architecture (Word ➔ HTML)
Our words must be as sharp as our numbers.

- **Structure**:
  1. **The Lead**: One-sentence high-conviction summary.
  2. **Investment Thesis**: The "Why Now?" (The Alpha).
  3. **Operational Moat**: Deep-dive into unit economics and competitive barriers.
  4. **Valuation**: DCF, SOTP, and Peer Benchmarking.
- **Analyst Call-outs**: 
  - Use `<div class="cs-analyst-note">`.
  - Feature a subtle Alpha Green glow and the analyst’s initials in a circular badge.

---

## 🚀 Phase 3: Deployment & SEO (The Final Polish)
Reports must be discoverable and shareable as professional assets.

- **URL Taxonomy**: `/research/[ticker]-[year]-[report-type].html`
- **Social Impact (LinkedIn/Twitter)**:
  - Generate a "Rich Preview" card.
  - **Title**: `Equity Research | [Company Name] ([Ticker]) | Capital Square`
  - **Description**: A high-impact snippet starting with "Rating: [BUY/SELL]..."
- **A4 Mastery**:
  - The "Download PDF" feature must respect `A4` dimensions exactly.
  - Force page breaks between major sections to prevent data splitting.
